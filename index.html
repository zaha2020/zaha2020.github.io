Biography

Welcome! I am a master’s student of Artificial intelligence and a Research Assistant at the Social Networks lab at the school of ECE at the University of Tehran. As an MSc student in AI who’s been surrounded nowadays by a great deal of data, I’ve found myself quite motivated to actively work and follow daily learning of Social Networks, Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Big Data, and Statistics. I have a true passion for learning different tools and everything which helps me get closer to applying deeply the data science.

In my research, I focus on predicting social indexes from Twitter. I’ve been fortunate to have a wonderful supervisor on this topic, including Dr. Masoud Asadpour.
